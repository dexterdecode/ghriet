About Frost

Frost Messenger is a free messaging app available. Frost uses your phone's Internet connection to let you message and call friends and family. Messaging is made fun with Frost, send and receive messages, calls, photos, gifs with complete privacy and a lot more.
Million's of Free GIF's - Ever got into a situation where you ran out of words or need something which can help you express right the way you feel, well say it with gifs! Share awesome, hilarious & fun gifs with your friends! With the largest collection of Gif's, express yourself better!
Introducing the new feature "Frost's World" which allows you say Hi to the world! or rather a little more than that. Scream out loud and let the world hear you at Frost's World.
Join the Frost community and express yourself.
* Free Voice and Video calls anywhere and anytime Place voice or video calls to your friends and family who are on Frost and stay in touch.
* Absolute Privacy. Users can add each other as friends via Unique Profile QR code to Ensure absolute Privacy. Frost does not use your phone number for communication but username. A phone number is never used for any communication making each user's privacy a major priority.
* Stay informed and notified with Frost. Never miss a notification of your friends and family messages.
* Express yourself. Send emojis, photos, and GIFs to conversations or just simple text use it in the way you feel it right
* Make international voice and video calls with Frost Place international calls to mobile phones with no additional charges. You can call all your friends who are on Frost.
*Data charges may apply. Contact your provider for details.
